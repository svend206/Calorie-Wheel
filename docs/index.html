Calorie Wheel

Simple, beautiful calorie tracking.

About

Calorie Wheel is a simple calorie tracker that lets you log your daily intake by rotating an interactive wheel. Set your daily goal, choose your increment, and watch the wheel change color as you approach your target.

Features

  • Interactive rotating wheel with haptic feedback
  • Color-coded progress (green → yellow → orange → red)
  • Customizable daily goal (500–10,000 calories)
  • Adjustable increment (10, 25, 50, or 100 calories per notch)
  • Home screen widget
  • Automatic daily reset at 3am
  • Dark theme throughout

Privacy Policy

Calorie Wheel does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information (calorie counts, goals, and settings) is stored locally on your device only. No data is shared with third parties. No analytics or tracking is used.

The app does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any external servers.
